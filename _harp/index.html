Intensely interested in the intricate

I'm a software geek from Ontario who loves tinkering with things and writing code.

I've always had a passion for the detailed and minute. Ever since I was little, I've been exploring what makes things work the way they do.

When I was 8, my family got its first computer. I wrote my first webpage in pure HTML a few months after, and I haven't looked back since. Browse some of my projects over on Github, or check out this page's source code, which is also hosted over on Github.

I'm a hard working programmer who is always focused on improving his skills, whether that means learning a new language, or improving my understanding of the language I'm using. In my leisure time, I like to study architecture, history and nature, and read voraciously.
