Intensely interested in the intricate

I'm a software geek who loves tinkering with things and writing code. Currently, I'm enrolled in the Computer Science program at Algonquin college.

I've been enthralled with computers ever since my dad brought home a Pentium III desktop when I was 8. It only took me a few months to start designing webpages and writing simple scripts. I love understanding how each individual piece works and fits into the greater whole.

Most of my web development experience is in front-end development using HTML, CSS, Javascript and JS frameworks. I also have experience with Java and Python, and I'm currently working on developing Android apps.

In my leisure time, I enjoy photography, drawing and reading. My favourite topics include architecture, history and nature. You can check out what I'm currently reading on Goodreads.
