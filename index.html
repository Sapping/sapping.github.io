About Me

I've always had a passion for the detailed and minute. Since I was little, my passion has been exploring what makes things work the way they do.

When I was 8, my family got its first computer. I wrote my first webpage in pure HTML a few months after, and I haven't looked back since. I have however added a few more tools to my skillset over the years, including CSS and Javascript, Python and Linux-based environments.

I'm a hard working programmer who is always focused on improving his skills, whether that means learning a new language, or improving my understanding of the language I'm using.

In my leisure time, I like to study architecture, history and nature, and read voraciously.
